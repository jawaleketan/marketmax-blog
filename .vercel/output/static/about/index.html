Welcome to MarketMax, your free resource for practical digital marketing knowledge. I'm Ketan Jawale, and I created this blog to share what I've learned about marketing in the digital age.

Why MarketMax?

The internet is full of overpriced courses and jargon-filled guides. MarketMax is my attempt to cut through the noise and deliver actionable, easy-to-understand marketing insights, completely free.

What I Cover

  • SEO: Technical SEO, keyword research, link building
  • Social Media: Platform strategies, content creation
  • Content Marketing: Copywriting, storytelling, distribution
  • PPC: Google Ads, budget optimization
  • Analytics: Data-driven decisions, reporting

My Philosophy

Marketing should be accessible to everyone. Whether you're a small business owner, a freelancer, or someone just starting, you shouldn't have to pay a fortune to learn what works.
