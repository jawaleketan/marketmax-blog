Build a Free Marketing Analytics Dashboard in 30 Minutes

Stop guessing and start knowing. Learn how to build a free marketing analytics dashboard using Google Looker Studio.

      Data is only useful if you can see it clearly. A marketing dashboard gives you a single view of what’s working and what’s not.

      Here’s how to build a free marketing analytics dashboard in 30 minutes.

      What You’ll Need

      • Google Looker Studio — Free
      • Google Analytics 4 — Free
      • Google Search Console — Free
      • Google Sheets — Free

      Step 1: Connect Your Data Sources

      Google Looker Studio connects directly to GA4 for traffic, Search Console for organic performance, YouTube Analytics, and Google Ads.

      Step 2: Define Your Key Metrics

      Track traffic metrics (sessions, users, sources), engagement metrics (session duration, pages/session, bounce rate), conversion metrics (goal completions, conversion rate), and SEO metrics (impressions, clicks, average position).

      Step 3: Build Your Dashboard Layout

      Organize with sections: Executive Summary → Traffic Overview → Content Performance → SEO Health → Conversions.

      Step 4: Use the Right Visualizations

      Time series for trends, bar charts for comparisons, scorecards for key numbers, tables for detailed data.

      Step 5: Set Up Automated Reports

      Schedule your dashboard to email stakeholders automatically daily, weekly, or monthly.

      The Bottom Line

      You don’t need expensive analytics tools. With Google Looker Studio, you can build a professional marketing dashboard in under 30 minutes — completely free.
